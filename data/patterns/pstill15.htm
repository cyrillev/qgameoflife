Alternate syntheses of block on long cis shillelagh (11.19):

Since the basic 6-glider synthesis of the long cis shillelagh (11.19) is extremely large, multiple syntheses variants cannot conveniently fit on one page. These are the five affected pseudo-still-lifes. Selecting the long cis shillelagh shows the synthesis in which it is created first, and then the block is added; selecting the block shows the synthesis in which the block is created first and the long cis shillelagh is added.

The block below long cis shillelagh can also be synthesized more cheaply by making both at the same time.
