c/2 Spaceships:

Most of the known Life spaceships (at least the small ones) move at a velocity of c/2. These have a base period of 4, but some of the larger variants have periods which are multiples of this.

H. Koenig's computer searches have not revealed any spaceships which were not previously known, but they have confirmed that the previously-known lists of small spaceships have been complete up to 24 bits.
