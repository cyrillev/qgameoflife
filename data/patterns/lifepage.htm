Object sizes are determined by the number of living cells in the smallest generation. Lists of still-lifes, pseudo-still-lifes, and period-2 oscillators and pseudo-oscillators have been generated or verified by computer search. Lists of larger-period oscillators and spaceships have been compiled and extrapolated from many sources, and tabulated by hand. Lists of larger-period pseudo-oscillators and pseudo-spaceships have been extrapolated from the above.

Lists of the 19-bit period-2 oscillators, 17- through 19-bit period-2 pseudo-oscillators (most of which can be easily synthesized), 18- through 24-bit still-lifes, and 16- through 24-bit pseudo-still-lifes are omitted due to lack of space.

Also, the following are currently missing from these pages:
  • Lists of 14-bit still-lifes currently omit syntheses. All of the 619 14-bit still-lifes can be synthesized, but only about 10% of the syntheses have been converted to a form usable by these pages. When they are all converted, they will be added.
  • Lists of 15-bit still-lifes currently omit syntheses. All but about 100 of the 1353 15-bit still-lifes can probably be synthesized using techniques used in smaller still-lifes, but only about 25% of the syntheses have been instantiated. When all of these (except the intractable ones) are finished, they will be added.
  • Lists of 18-bit period 2 oscillators currently omit syntheses. I have no plans to synthesize these in the near future.
  • Syntheses of miscellaneous still-lifes and pseudo-still-lifes larger than 15 bits: I have no plans to systematically synthesize these in the near future. However, I have around 400 miscellanous syntheses, which will be added as time and space permit.
  • Syntheses of miscellaneous larger-period oscillators: I have several more of these, which will be added as time permits.
